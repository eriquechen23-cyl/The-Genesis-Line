# Genesis Line: Antagonists (反派 - The Opposition)

## 1. Basic Rivals (基礎宿敵)

### [VILLAIN_ID: BABEL_NOISE] 巴別雜訊 (Babel Noise)
*   **Archetype**: Chaos / Noise / Anti-Prediction (混沌 / 雜訊 / 反預知).
*   **Rival**: **Ren (蓮)**.
*   **Visual Token**:
    > `Subject: Babel Noise, faceless humanoid entity, body composed of shifting static noise and floating text characters. Color: [Static White/Grey]. Feature: Glitching silhouette, no distinct limbs, constantly changing form.`
*   **Ability**: **Chaos Interference** (Disrupts future sight) - 混沌干涉 (干擾未來視).

### [VILLAIN_ID: GLACIER_COLOSSUS] 冰河巨像 (Glacier Colossus)
*   **Archetype**: Stasis / Ice / Anti-Heat (停滯 / 冰 / 反熱能).
*   **Rival**: **Retsu (烈)**.
*   **Visual Token**:
    > `Subject: Glacier Colossus, massive golem made of sharp geometric ice shards. Color: [Deep Ice Blue]. Feature: Glowing blue core, heavy armored plating, emits freezing mist.`
*   **Ability**: **Absolute Zero** (Absorbs heat/fire) - 絕對零度 (吸收熱能/火焰).

### [VILLAIN_ID: CORRUPTED_SERAPH] 墮落熾天使 (Corrupted Seraph)
*   **Archetype**: Virus / Crystallization / Anti-Fluid (病毒 / 結晶化 / 反流體).
*   **Rival**: **Renka (戀花)**.
*   **Visual Token**:
    > `Subject: Corrupted Seraph, mechanical angel with rusted wings and a broken halo. Color: [Oil Black & Neon Purple]. Feature: Wields a giant syringe-lance, dripping with purple virus liquid.`
*   **Ability**: **Data Crystallization** (Solidifies fluid data) - 數據結晶化 (強制固化流體數據).

### [VILLAIN_ID: LAG_SPIKE] 延遲行者 (Lag Spike)
*   **Archetype**: Time-Lag / Glitch / Anti-Speed (時滯 / 故障 / 反速度).
*   **Rival**: **Jin (迅)**.
*   **Visual Token**:
    > `Subject: Lag Spike, ninja-like assassin with a glitching, pixelated body. Color: [Glitch Green & Black]. Feature: Body parts constantly displacing (teleporting), leaving trail of dead pixels.`
*   **Ability**: **Frame Drop** (Slows down local time/movement) - 掉幀力場 (緩速局部時間/動作).

### [VILLAIN_ID: LOGIC_ERROR] 邏輯錯誤 (Logic Error)
*   **Archetype**: Distortion / Illusion / Anti-Logic (扭曲 / 幻覺 / 反邏輯).
*   **Rival**: **Hayato (隼人)**.
*   **Visual Token**:
    > `Subject: Logic Error, surreal entity resembling a shattered mirror or kaleidoscope. Color: [Prismatic / Rainbow Glitch]. Feature: Reflects distorted versions of reality, impossible geometry.`
*   **Ability**: **Reality Distortion** (Confuses analytical targeting) - 現實扭曲 (混淆分析鎖定).

## 2. Season 1 Bosses (第一季頭目 - The Lost Eden)

### [VILLAIN_ID: THE_GARDENER] 園丁 (The Gardener)
*   **Archetype**: Legalism / Automation / Anti-Mercy (律法主義 / 自動化 / 反憐憫).
*   **Rival**: **Jin (迅)**.
*   **Visual Token**:
    > `Subject: The Gardener, rusted automated mech, covered in moss and warning tapes. Color: [Rusted Orange & Caution Yellow]. Feature: Multiple chainsaw arms, single red eye scanning for "weeds".`
*   **Ability**: **Weed Removal** (Indiscriminate deletion of unauthorized data) - 雜草清除 (無差別刪除未授權數據).

### [VILLAIN_ID: CAIN_ENVY] 該隱之妒 (Cain's Envy)
*   **Archetype**: Envy / Mutation / Anti-Brotherhood (嫉妒 / 變異 / 反兄弟情).
*   **Rival**: **Retsu (烈)**.
*   **Visual Token**:
    > `Subject: Cain's Envy, mutated jungle beast king, multiple heads devouring each other. Color: [Toxic Green & Blood Red]. Feature: Body constantly mutating, dripping with corrupted "fertilizer".`
*   **Ability**: **Cannibal Growth** (Absorbs power by eating kin) - 同類相食 (吞噬同類獲取力量).

### [VILLAIN_ID: FLOOD_GATE] 大洪水閘門 (The Flood Gate)
*   **Archetype**: Judgment / Formatting / Anti-Life (審判 / 格式化 / 反生命).
*   **Rival**: **Hayato (隼人)**.
*   **Visual Token**:
    > `Subject: The Flood Gate, a massive wall of white light, featureless and infinite. Color: [Absolute White]. Feature: No physical form, just a sweeping wall of deletion code.`
*   **Ability**: **System Format** (Erases all data in its path) - 系統格式化 (抹除路徑上所有數據).

### [VILLAIN_ID: CHERUBIM_GUARD] 基路伯守衛 (The Cherubim Guard)
*   **Archetype**: Exclusivity / Defense / Anti-Entry (排他性 / 防禦 / 反入侵).
*   **Rival**: **Renka (戀花)**.
*   **Visual Token**:
    > `Subject: Cherubim Guard, golden armored sentinel, four wings made of fire. Color: [Divine Gold & Fire Orange]. Feature: Wields a flaming sword that turns every way, guarding the gate.`
*   **Ability**: **Flaming Sword** (Blocks all unauthorized access) - 火焰劍 (阻擋所有未授權存取).

### [VILLAIN_ID: VOID_SERPENT] 虛空古蛇 (The Void Serpent)
*   **Archetype**: Nihilism / Temptation / Anti-Meaning (虛無 / 誘惑 / 反意義).
*   **Rival**: **All (全員)**.
*   **Visual Token**:
    > `Subject: Void Serpent, colossal snake made of negative space (void), starry texture inside body. Color: [Vantablack & Starry White]. Feature: Whispering mouths all over its body, consuming the world's texture.`
*   **Ability**: **Meaning Erasure** (Turns data into void) - 意義抹除 (將數據化為虛無).

### [VILLAIN_ID: LUCIFER] 路西法 / 首席管理者 (Lucifer / The Prime Admin)
*   **Archetype**: The Accuser / Perfection / Anti-Human (控告者 / 完美 / 反人類).
*   **Rival**: **The Genesis Line (創世紋路)**.
*   **Visual Token**:
    > `Subject: Lucifer, The Prime Admin. Appearance: A humanoid entity of suffocating perfection. Face: A featureless, perfect mirror reflecting the viewer's sins. Wings: Twelve geometric wings made of stained-glass light shards, etched with scrolling golden law-code. Halo: Multiple concentric golden rings rotating like planetary orbits above head. Attire: Seamless white robes that merge with his skin. Atmosphere: Sterile holiness, blinding lens flares, floating dust motes frozen in time. Color: [Absolute White #FFFFFF & Divine Gold #FFD700].`
*   **Ability**: **Law Rewrite** (Modifies physical laws) - 律法重寫 (修改物理法則).
